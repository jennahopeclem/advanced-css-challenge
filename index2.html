About Me

My name is Jenna and I am an aspiring web designer and developer. I graduated from Rutgers University in 2019 with a degree in Communication and a minor in Digital Communication and Information Media (DCIM). Post-grad I got a job working for a small marketing agency, D2 Creative as a Content and Media specialist.

During my time in the field, I fell in love with using basic HTML and CSS to build and maintain client websites. After getting laid off, the Rutgers Coding Bootcamp fell into my lap and I felt it was time to take the leap. For fun, I enjoy gaming, reading, and hanging with my two kittens.
